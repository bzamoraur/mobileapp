# The 5-minute live demo — script & choreography

*The demo sells this product; the words don't. Your job is to make the advisor feel the three things no competitor delivers: **it installs like an app**, **it works with no signal**, and **it's their brand**. Do it on a real phone whenever possible.*

## The demo pack (what you show)

| # | App | Shows | Link |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Safari (Tanzania & Zanzíbar)** | Luxury/adventure, the "fauna" tracker, real flights/hotels | `[your live Tanzania URL]` |
| 2 | **City (Tokio)** | A totally different theme + a non-Latin phrasebook with audio | `[your live viaje-japan URL]` |
| 3 | **Beach (clean demo)** | The beach theme + a "things to tick off" collection, **no personal data** — your default cold-demo | `[deploy the demo-pack beach trip]` |

> For cold prospects, lead with **#3 (no personal data)**. For warm/your-own-network, **#1** carries the authentic "I built this for my own family's safari" story.

## Before the call (30 seconds of prep)
- Have the 3 links open on your **phone**, and a backup on screen-share.
- Pre-install one to your home screen so you can show the icon.
- Know one trip *they* sell (to say "imagine this is your [Maldives honeymoon]").

## The choreography (≈5 min)

**0:00 — Frame it (15s).** "En vez de contártelo, te lo enseño. Esto es lo que recibiría tu cliente."

**0:15 — Install it (45s) — the first wow.** Open link on the phone → browser menu → **"Añadir a pantalla de inicio"** → show the **icon on the home screen** → open it fullscreen. *"Sin tienda de apps, sin registro. Un enlace o un QR y ya está en su móvil."*

**1:00 — Airplane mode (45s) — the killer wow.** Turn on **airplane mode** → reopen the app → navigate the itinerary, the map, the phrasebook. *"Estamos sin datos ni wifi — como tu cliente en pleno [Serengeti / isla]. Sigue funcionando todo."* **This is the moment that closes advisors. Always do it.**

**1:45 — Walk the trip (90s).** Home → today's plan → a day's detail → the **map** (tap a pin → it opens Google Maps) → **Frases** → press **▶ audio** (hear it speak) → the **collection** (tick a monument/animal → it saves). Keep it snappy; let them hold the phone.

**3:15 — Make it theirs (45s).** "Y todo esto va con **tu marca** — tu logo, tus colores, tus datos de contacto." Show #1 vs #3 to prove it re-skins entirely. If you built a Trojan-horse demo of *their* trip, open it now: *"De hecho… te monté el tuyo."*

**4:00 — The offer + the ask (60s).** "Te hago el de tu próximo cliente en **48 h**. El primero **gratis** por tu feedback; luego 49 €/viaje o 99 €/mes. ¿Tienes un viaje saliendo pronto que pueda montar esta semana?" → **get a specific trip + the booking PDF**.

## Do / Don't
- ✅ Do the airplane-mode trick. Always. It's the demo.
- ✅ Put the phone in their hands — let them install it themselves.
- ✅ Anchor on a trip they actually sell.
- ❌ Don't talk tech (PWA, schema, Cloudflare). Talk **client experience**.
- ❌ Don't show the Tanzania app's personal data to a *competitor* advisor — use #3.
- ❌ Don't leave without either a booking doc or a booked next step.

## If it's async (no call)
Send the **#3 link** + this line: *"Ábrelo en el móvil → 'Añadir a pantalla de inicio' → ponlo en modo avión y verás que sigue funcionando. Esto, con tu marca, para tus clientes. ¿Te monto uno gratis?"* + the QR image.
